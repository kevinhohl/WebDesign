This website aims to provide information for current residents and potential visitors about the southport neighborhood. This website will cover most aspects of southport including transport, accomodation, facilities and shops that a person will be able to visit.

Introduction to Southport:

Southport is both a suburb and business district in the center of Gold Coast, Australia. It is located just to the north of Surfers Paradise and has the largest communities of the city. Southport was named so because it was the most southern point of the Queensland colony.
