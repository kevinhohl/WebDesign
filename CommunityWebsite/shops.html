Australia Fair Shopping Mall

Australia Fair Mall is the biggest shopping mall in Southport. It is located right by the highway on Marine Parade and is very close to the Schools, Hospital and Residential areas. Here you will be able to satisfy all your purchasing needs. The rest of this page will highlight some of the hundreds of places you can visit in the Australia Fair Mall.

Woolworths

Woolworths is the place to go for all your grocery and everyday needs. They have a wide selection of meat, snacks, condiments and other items available for quick and easy purchase.

Fruits and Vegetables

Australia fair has its own fresh fruit and vegetables stand where you can find only the freshest hand picked fruits and vegetables from all over Queensland available for purchase.

Coffee Shops

Take a break from shopping and have a fresh cup of brewed coffee at one of the many coffee shops found inside Australia Fair mall. The one pictured here is Zaraffas Coffee where they make delicious cafe products.

Chemists

Terry White chemists at Australia Fair will provide you with only the best quality goods from shampoos and makeups to vitamins and medicines and other health care products

Bottle Shop

This little bottle shop located inside Australia Fair has a wide variety of Alcohol for your recreational or partying needs. (18+ only, ID may need to be presented before purchase)
