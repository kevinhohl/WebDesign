Southport Facilities

Southport is the central business district in Gold Coast, Australia so it is sure to have only the best facilities around. From Schools, Hospitals and Sports fields you will find only the best places for your health and educational needs.

Central Queensland University

Central Queensland University's Gold Coast campus is the main University you will find in Southport. It is located only a few minutes walk from the Australia Fair Shopping mall, right beside the Highway on Marine Parade street. They offer a variety of courses from Accounting, Business to Information Technology and have only the best teachers and is equipped with the latest technologies.

Gold Coast Hospital

The Gold Coast hospital is located on Nerang Street in Southport. It is a short walk away from the Schools and the Mall as well as the residential areas. It is equipped with the latest medical technology and staff that offer health care on a daily basis to ensure all your medical needs are met perfectly.

Sports Field

Exercise plays a big part in every persons life. In Southport you will find a huge sports field with tons of space for you to play soccer or football. It is also possible to use the field for running and jogging as there is plenty of space to accomodate everyones needs.
